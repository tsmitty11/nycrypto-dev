Introducing Tidus Wallet

A cutting-edge, secure, and user-centric decentralized wallet, meticulously designed to offer an intuitive experience for new comers and power users.

With Tidus, you can swap, stake, lend, and borrow effortlessly—all within the app on any chain. No more endless clicks or clunky interfaces. Just smooth, hassle-free defi on your terms.

Effortlessly on ramp to any blockchain without the hassle of bridging your assets—because who has time for that? Buy and sell crypto in a few taps with Tidus, making crypto as smooth as it should be.

Tidus is a non-custodial wallet, meaning we can't touch your funds, ever. But we get it—those seed phrases can be a headache. That’s why we’ve added social recovery, securely stored on your device. So you’re always in control, with a little extra peace of mind.

Tidus is already easy to use, but we’ve made it even simpler. See those orange “i” icons sprinkled around the app? Whenever you're curious or need a quick refresher, just tap one. You’ll get straightforward explanations and helpful links—no jargon, just clear info to keep you in the know.
